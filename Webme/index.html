Eagle Eye

Eagle Eye is an android application which makes the world secure that help the customers to safe their affluence. This will solve the home burglary problem when people are not in home by using two smart phones with our application.One mobile is placed in home. Another mobile will be with user. Sound has been detected from home then home mobile will send a alert message to User. User can take action immediately from any place. User can see the Photos and LIve video of their home if the thief come or not. After Confirmation they can instruct the home phone to call the police. Home mobile will take any action when it gets permission from the user.
